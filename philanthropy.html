Alpha Chi Omega

Philanthropy

Our national philanthropy is Domestic Violence Awareness (DVA). Locally, at our Theta Chapter, we support the Women's Center of Southeastern Michigan to fund and donate feminine hygeine products for victims of domestic violence. We also educate our members on the importance of healthy relationships in all aspects of life. A central theme we focus on is defining what we think healthy love is.

Fundraising Events

Most of our philanthropy events throughout the year revolve around raising money to donate to the Women's Center, since we cannot actually go visit the center due to respecting the privacy of the patients. This past year, some of the fundraisers at our house have been a hot chocolate bar, a pancake breakfast, and partnering with Joe's Pizza. We also hold an event near Valentine's Day to create care packages to send to the Women's Center.
